So, any one-pot pasta recipe you see here on my website is saucy and oozy.

Directions

  1. Thaw & Prep Shrimp
    • Place frozen shrimp in a bowl, cover with cold water, and let sit 15-20 minutes (swap water once halfway).
    • Drain and pat very dry with paper towels.
  2. Marinate Shrimp
    • In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, cumin, red pepper flakes, salt & pepper.
    • Let sit 10 minutes while you prep the other ingredients.
  3. Saut√© Onions
    • In a large (12‚Ä≥) skillet over medium heat, melt the vegan butter.
    • Add diced onion and cook, stirring often, until translucent (3-4 minutes).
  4. Toast the Orzo
    • Add the dry orzo to the softened onions.
    • Toast, stirring, until the pasta is lightly golden (3-5 minutes).
  5. Saut√© Garlic & Peppers
    • Stir in minced garlic and the diced red & green bell peppers.
    • Cook 1-2 minutes until fragrant and peppers begin to soften.
  6. Simmer the Orzo
    • Pour in chicken stock and bring to a boil.
    • Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until orzo is tender and most liquid is absorbed.
    • Stir in the granulated bouillon; add extra stock if needed for creaminess.
  7. Add Shrimp & Veggies
    • Nestle the marinated shrimp (and any juices) into the orzo.
    • Stir in the frozen mixed vegetables.
    • Cover and cook 5-7 minutes, until shrimp are opaque and veggies are heated through.
  8. Finish & Serve
    • Remove from heat and, if desired, drizzle melted vegan butter on top.
    • Serve immediately and enjoy!

Notes

  1. If cooking in a pot, spray generously with non-stick cooking spray before heating to prevent sticking.
